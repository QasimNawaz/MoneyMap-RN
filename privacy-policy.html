Privacy Policy for MoneyMap

Last updated: April 17, 2024

Introduction

MoneyMap ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data when you use our expense management application.

Information We Collect

Our app operates entirely on your device and collects only the following information:

  • Transaction data you manually enter
  • Account information you create within the app
  • Categories and labels you create
  • Device settings and preferences

How We Use Your Information

All data is stored locally on your device. We do not:

  • Collect or transmit your financial data
  • Share your information with third parties
  • Use your data for marketing purposes
  • Track your location or behavior

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely on your device using SQLite database encryption. We do not store any of your information on external servers.

Data Backup

Any backups of your data are managed through your device's built-in backup systems. We recommend regularly backing up your device to prevent data loss.

Third-Party Services

Our app does not integrate with any third-party services that would have access to your data.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect or maintain information from persons under 13 years of age.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.

Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control over your information. You can:

  • Access all your data within the app
  • Modify or delete any information
  • Clear all data through your device's app settings
